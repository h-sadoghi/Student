Research and Technology Background and Introduction to Specialized Lab#

Dr. Hadi Sadoghi Yazdi’s research background is in the areas of pattern recognition, machine learning, computer vision, signal processing. He established the specialized Pattern Recognition Lab with the aim of conducting research and technological activities. He is the director and manager of the lab.

Educational Background#

Dr. Hadi Sadoghi Yazdi graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering - Electronics from Ferdowsi University of Mashhad, and received his master’s and Ph.D. degrees from Tarbiat Modares University. He has been a faculty member in the Department of Computer Engineering at the Faculty of Engineering, Ferdowsi University, since 2007.

Product Title: Interactive Smart Mirror#

Brief Description:#

Detailed Feature Description:#

The Interactive Smart Mirror is a cutting-edge product designed to enhance user interaction by displaying various types of information directly on the mirror’s surface. This innovative mirror can show text content such as daily news, weather updates, and calendar events. It also monitors and displays environmental conditions, including temperature and humidity, making it an ideal addition to smart homes and modern facilities.

With its built-in IoT platform, the mirror can connect with other smart devices, allowing for seamless integration into existing smart home ecosystems. The touch interaction feature enables users to navigate through different menus and options displayed on the mirror, providing an intuitive and user-friendly experience.

On the technical side, the Interactive Smart Mirror incorporates advanced hardware development, including high-resolution display panels and responsive touch sensors. The product also features sophisticated image processing capabilities through an integrated camera, as well as audio processing to respond to voice commands.

The Interactive Smart Mirror is suitable for installation in various locations, including hotels and the Holy Shrine of Imam Reza, offering guests and visitors a unique and technologically advanced experience.

Product Title: Interactive Smart Wall#

Brief Description:#

User interaction and display response on the wall.

Product Images:#

Product Features Description:#

In this section, you can describe the product features either in list format (maximum 12 items, each up to 12 words) or in a descriptive format (maximum 200 words).

Capabilities:#

  • Games and entertainment

  • Education

  • Interactive advertising

Technical Knowledge:#

  • 3D image processing

  • Object processing and color recognition

  • Skeleton estimation

  • Estimation of the position of people and objects

Execution Location:#

  • Municipality of Mashhad

  • Exhibitions

  • Ferdowsi University

Detailed Feature Description:#

The Interactive Smart Wall is an innovative product designed to enhance user interaction by responding to actions and displaying various types of content on the wall. This advanced wall can be used for multiple purposes, including gaming and entertainment, educational applications, and interactive advertising.

With its 3D image processing capabilities, the Interactive Smart Wall can accurately track and respond to user movements, providing an immersive experience. It can recognize objects and colors, estimate the skeletal position of users, and determine the location of people and objects such as balls, making it ideal for interactive games and educational demonstrations.

In the technical domain, the wall incorporates advanced hardware and software to process and display high-quality 3D images. Its sophisticated image processing algorithms enable real-time interaction, making it suitable for dynamic environments like exhibitions and public spaces.

The Interactive Smart Wall is versatile and can be installed in various locations, including the Municipality of Mashhad, exhibitions, and Ferdowsi University. It offers an engaging and interactive platform for users, making it an excellent addition to any modern facility.
